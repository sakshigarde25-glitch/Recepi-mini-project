Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is creamy, rich, and absolutely delicious. Made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper, it's a comforting meal that's quick to prepare. Perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Pecorino Romano cheese (plus extra for serving)
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the pancetta until crispy, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti. Quickly toss the hot spaghetti with the pancetta in the skillet.
  5. Remove from heat and immediately pour in the egg mixture, stirring vigorously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time if needed to loosen the sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper on top. Enjoy!
